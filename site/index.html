What We're Building

One platform for the entire
independent music ecosystem

The live music industry runs on scattered tools, crushing fees, and zero transparency. Ticketmaster doesn't care about your 300-cap room. Eventbrite is a checkout page pretending to be a platform. Nobody's building for the clubs, the bands, and the fans who actually make this scene work. We are.

🎟️

Fair Ticketing

Under $1.25 total fees on a $15 ticket. Not a typo. Venues pay a flat monthly rate, fans pay almost nothing, and bands actually get what they're owed through automated, transparent settlement.

🤝

Connected

Profiles for bands, venues, bookers, labels, and fans. Fill last-minute slots instantly. Route tours through the venue network. Discover new music through actual attendance, not algorithms.

📹

Archived

Every set recorded, broken out by band, synced with fan-shot video. A living archive of every show. Your ticket unlocks the full set. Your concert history builds itself.
